Description: Make Pixa play music on Raspberry Pi 3

Steps:

+ Install MyMedia for Raspberry Pi at https://www.mymediaalexa.com/

+ Extract the downloaded MyMedia zip and select "pi" for identify and enter "raspberry" for password

+ Install your music files into your Pi's SD card

+ Go to http://localhost:52051 after finishing MyMedia installing

+ Click "Next" on the main page, and sign in with your Amazon account

+ Click Watch Folder, and select the folder that you installed your music for the Pi earlier, then upload your music

+ Go to https://alexa.amazon.com/spa/index.html and search MyMedia under Skills tab

+ Enable MyMedia skill with the same Amazon account that you log into MyMedia

+ Now you can then say "Pixa, ask MyMedia to play music"
